## Business Processes

### Todo Management

**Value**: Users can log in with just a username and manage their personal todo items. Login is the entry point; CRUD comes later.

**Steps**:
1. User visits the home page and enters their username
2. User is logged in and sees a welcome message
3. *(Future: User creates, views, updates, and deletes todos)*

## User Personas

### User
Manages their own todo items. Authenticated via NextAuth credentials.
